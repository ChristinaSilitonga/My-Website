About

My Story

I am a UX Designer with over 13 years of experience and training in linguistics, finance, entrepreneurship, management and liaison. I have a passion for learning new things that enrich and satisfy my high level of curiosity to achieve something in the development of my knowledge and skills. Since my passion is observation, I like to analyze and find things that can be used as solutions, based of course on deliberation and the development of creative ideas. I have strong empathy and can put myself in other people's shoes. These things can certainly be my strengths as a UX designer who is able to design useful features, work well in layout and navigation, easy to use and of course nice to look at with a user interface approach.

My former colleagues know me as a person who is very committed to completing a project, is able to cope with the risks that arise, to be competitive, full of self-confidence with skills, extensive knowledge and professional experience of more than 10 years in this field of financial management and liaison. With a proven track record as a designer who is tough, dedicated, responsible, good at communicating and able to appear confident.
